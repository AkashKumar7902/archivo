What is DBpedia Archivo?

DBpedia Archivo is an online interface and augmented archive for all kinds of vocabularies. It automatically crawls for new ontologies, updates the already enlisted ontologies regularly and performs some useful tests to check the fitness of the vocabulary for the semantic web.

Archivo's mission

Archivo's mission is to improve FAIRness (findability, accessibility, interoperability, and reusability) of all available ontologies on the Semantic Web. Archivo is not a guideline, it is fully automated, machine-readable and enforces interoperability with its star rating. Ontology developers can implement against Archivo until they reach more stars. Ontology users can better find, access and re-use ontologies.

DBpedia Archivo was created as a scientific proof-of-concept and is currently being developed into a full platform.
